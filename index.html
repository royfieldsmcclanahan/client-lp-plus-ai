Increase Driver Retention with Enhanced ADAS Safety Technology from Plus

It’s no secret that driver turnover is a huge challenge right now.

American Trucking Associations estimates that in 2022 turnover is already as high as 89% for large fleets and 77% for smaller carriers.1 As the job market has gotten more competitive, drivers have more opportunities to drive for other fleets. How can you reduce your driver turnover and retain drivers longer?

Having a strong culture of safety is key to reducing driver turnover,2 and one way you can reinforce that safety culture is by providing your drivers with the industry’s most advanced safety technology for semi trucks.

At Plus, we have applied our autonomous driving technology to create an enhanced ADAS system that provides a 360-degree view around the truck, thereby improving driver safety and enhancing the overall driver experience. This is made possible by equipping semi trucks with PlusDrive, which combines best-in-class sensors and computing hardware with proprietary autonomous driving software and AI models for ultimate real-time awareness and control of a truck.

While the driver remains in control of the truck and stays in the vehicle as a supervisor, PlusDrive can automatically perform the following functions in normal traffic conditions on a highway:

  • Lane centering

  • Driver initiated lane change

  • Traffic jam assist

  • Predictive fuel optimization

  • Adaptive cruise control (down to zero)

  • Lane merging

  • Driver attentiveness detection

  • Self-calibration

  • Continuous software updates over-the-air

  • Much more!
